About the creator of the show

His influences

A lot of Alex Hirsch's inspirations stemed from his childhood.Like for example,on the show, there are many monsters and magical beings.The inspiration for this as Alex Hirsch says was from his love of monsters and the supernatural during his childhood.When he was a child,he collected books on the paranormal and the unknown and loved The X-Files.His inspiration for the twins' relationship was from his own relationship with his sister.

His career

Hirsch's first job after graduating from CalArts was as a writer and storyboard artist for The Marvelous Misadventures of Flapjack on Cartoon Network where he worked alongside fellow CalArts alumni, J. G. Quintel, Pendleton Ward (who was his writing partner on the show), and Patrick He also served as a creative consultant and helped develop the hit Disney Channel animated series, Fish Hooks, and voices the character Clamantha in the series. Hirsch chose Oregon as the setting for Gravity Falls after spending a summer there during college working for the animation studio Laika.

His involvement in the show

As the creator of the show, Alex Hirsch came up with the concept of the show. He also voiced the characters of Grunkle Stan,Bill Cipher,Old man Mcgucket, Dippie Fresh and the gnomes.

What he is currently up to

He is currently the voice of King, Hooty, and additional voices in the Disney Channel animated series The Owl House, created by Dana Terrace (Hirsch's longtime partner). The show premiered on January 10, 2020.
